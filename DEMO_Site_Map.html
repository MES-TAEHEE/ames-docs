Concept
The A-MES demo site reorganizes the 11 design-spec modules into 3 demonstrable systems by usage environment, so each can be shown to its real audience on its real device. Office Web runs on desktop browsers for planners and engineers; Shop-Floor POP runs on fixed touch terminals beside the line; Logistics PDA runs on handheld scanners in the warehouse. Each screen below is a working interactive demo with realistic sample data based on a U.S. automotive parts plant operation.
