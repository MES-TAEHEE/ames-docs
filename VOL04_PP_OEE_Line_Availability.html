01
Overview
화면 개요 · 목적 · 컨텍스트
Rolls up the classified PP_LineStateLog into a time-loss model and computes OEE per line and period. Loading Time (Σ planned OPERATING from MD-29) minus PLANNED_DOWN and UNPLANNED_DOWN yields the Operating Time and Availability; combined with Performance (cycle-time efficiency) and Quality (QC yield) it produces OEE. The screen separates planned vs unplanned loss so each line's own time pattern is fairly credited.
분류된 PP_LineStateLog를 시간손실 모델로 롤업하여 라인·기간별 OEE를 산출한다. 부하시간(MD-29 계획 OPERATING 합계)에서 PLANNED_DOWN·UNPLANNED_DOWN을 차감해 가동시간과 가용성을 구하고, 성능(사이클타임 효율)·품질(QC 수율)과 결합해 OEE를 산출한다. 계획/비계획 손실을 분리 표기하여 각 라인 고유 시간패턴이 공정하게 반영된다.

RPT-06 OEE · RPT-07 KPI
Loading Time = Σ MD-29 OPERATING minutes  |  Operating Time = Loading − Planned Down − Unplanned Down
Availability = Operating Time ÷ Loading Time    Performance = (Ideal cycle × Qty) ÷ Operating Time
Quality = Good Qty ÷ Total Qty   →   OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality

04
Event & Action Flow
이벤트 흐름 · 롤업 산출
1
USERselect · period + line
User picks a period and line; the screen queries PP_LineStateLog minutes within the window.
기간·라인 선택 → 윈도우 내 PP_LineStateLog 분(分) 조회.
APIGET /api/pp/line-oee?from=&to=&line=
2
SYSTEMaggregate · time-loss model
Sums minutes by state, derives Loading / Operating Time and Availability per line.
상태별 분 합산 → 라인별 부하·가동시간·가용성 도출.
CALCAvailability = Operating ÷ Loading
3
SYSTEMjoin · performance + quality
Joins WO output (ideal cycle vs actual) and QC good-qty to compute Performance and Quality, then OEE.
WO 실적(이상 사이클 대비 실제)·QC 양품수량을 결합해 성능·품질·OEE 산출.
CALCOEE = A × P × Q
4
USERclick · [Calculate]
Persists a PP_LineOEE snapshot row per line for the period so the figure is auditable and stable.
기간별 라인 PP_LineOEE 스냅샷 저장 — 수치 감사·고정.
APIPOST /api/pp/line-oee/snapshot
5
SCHEDULEDnightly · auto-rollup
A nightly job snapshots the prior day's OEE and pushes it to RPT-06 and RPT-07.
야간 배치가 전일 OEE를 스냅샷하여 RPT-06·RPT-07로 전달.
DOWNSTREAMRPT-06 Equipment OEE · RPT-07 Monthly KPI
