01
Overview
화면 개요 · 목적 · 컨텍스트
Tracks On-Time Delivery (OTD) KPI by customer (SAV/GEO), item, and period. Calculates OTD% = (Shipped on or before due / Total shipped) × 100. Targets: SAV ≥ 98%, GEO ≥ 96%. Shows trend chart (12-month), delay root-cause Pareto, and individual delayed shipment drill-down. Feeds RPT-05 monthly KPI dashboard.
거래선(SAV/GEO)·품목·기간별 OTD KPI 추적. OTD% = (납기 내 출하 / 총 출하) × 100. 목표: SAV ≥ 98% · GEO ≥ 96%. 12개월 추이 차트·지연 원인 Pareto·개별 지연건 드릴다운. RPT-05 월간 KPI 대시보드 연계.

04
Event & Action Flow
이벤트 흐름 · 사용자 액션 → 시스템 반응
1
USER page.load · open OTD
Loads current period OTD with all filters at default. Calls aggregate API.
현재 기간 OTD를 기본 필터로 로드. 집계 API 호출.
APIGET /api/pp/otd?period=current_quarter
2
SYSTEM calc · OTD%
Aggregates: FG_Shipment joined with PP_WO. Counts where actual_ship_date ≤ wo_due_date.
FG_Shipment × PP_WO 조인. actual_ship_date ≤ wo_due_date 건수 집계.
FORMULAOTD% = COUNT(on_time) / COUNT(*) * 100
3
USER click · trend bar
Drills into selected month. Re-fetches detailed shipments for that month.
추이 차트 막대 클릭 시 해당 월 상세 출하 데이터 조회.
APIGET /api/pp/otd/detail?month=YYYY-MM
4
USER click · Pareto row
Drills into shipments with that delay reason. Shows root cause analysis link to QC/MNT data.
Pareto 행 클릭 시 해당 사유 지연건 목록 + QC/MNT 데이터 링크.
NAV/pp/otd/detail?cause=MaterialShortage
5
SCHEDULED cron · daily KPI snapshot
Every day at 00:30, snapshots OTD for previous day into PP_OTD_Snapshot.
매일 00:30 전일 OTD 스냅샷을 PP_OTD_Snapshot에 저장.
EFFECTUsed for fast trend rendering
6
SYSTEM feed · monthly KPI
Aggregated to RPT-05 monthly KPI dashboard. Director-level visibility.
월간 RPT-05 KPI 대시보드에 집계 전달. 임원진 가시성.
DOWNSTREAMRPT-05 · Email digest to plant director on miss
